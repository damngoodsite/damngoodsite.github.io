STEP
1

Fill out an online form

We keep things simple and cost-effective by skipping in-person meetings and using a self-service questionnaire instead. This form helps us understand what your site needs to do and how it should look. By streamlining this step, our small team can stay focused on delivering top-notch websites quickly and affordably.

STEP
2

website design creation

After you complete the questionnaire, we jump straight into designing your website. Using your answers, we craft a blueprint tailored to your industry with our proven design system. By reusing effective design components, we save time and avoid starting from scratch for every project. Need a fully custom site with unique features? We can do that too, but it’s a separate service with a custom quote. For most businesses, our streamlined system offers the perfect blend of quality and value.

STEP
3

website design review

Once the design is ready, we’ll send you a preview image—a “screenshot from the future.” This image gives you a clear picture of how your site will look and function, allowing us to make any necessary tweaks before moving to development. It’s a fast and efficient way to ensure the design is exactly what you need.

STEP
4

website development

After you approve the design, we get to work building your site. Thanks to our design system, we can complete the development process quickly—what usually takes weeks can now be done in just a few hours. This efficiency translates into major cost savings that we pass on to you.

STEP
5

live website approval

When development is complete, our quality control team tests your site to ensure everything works perfectly. We’ll then send you a link to review. Once you’re happy with the final product, your website will go live on your domain, ready to serve your business 24/7 around the globe.4o

FAQs

Need a few more things cleared up check below for some of our most frequently asked questions and some answers to those questions.

But How Much Is it?

The Base Damn Good Site is just $500 Out The Door. This includes the design and the build. We deliver a customized website design for your brand or business and 1 round of changes prior to publishing. Once the site is live and ready to go all updates are subject to an hourly "website change rate" that is submitted through our website change request form and typically completed within 48 hours. If you do not have a custom domain that will be an additional small fee (typically $12-$50/yr) to purchase and setup if you need assistance doing so.

Is There Really No Contract?

Yes! There is NO Contract. Once you pay the $500 at checkout and complete the onboarding form, we go into design creation. After approval it's then uploaded to our free hosting platform where it will be live on the internet. We then deliver a download link to your website files you can access at anytime if needed years down the road.

Can I Add Stuff To The Site?

Incorporating updates to elements such as images, services, and pricing is no problem and is done through a website change request form. If you're considering structural alterations, think of updates as painting a room and structural website changes like adding a section as build a wall. The complexity of the change dictates the process, we can anything you want it will be billed at a website change request hourly rate.

Will I Be On First Page Of Google?

It's quite improbable that you'll appear on Google's first page without months of your site being active and deliberate content development both on and off your website to improve your ranking. We consistently stay updated on search engine changes and work to enhance the site's performance in relation to them. We can offer SEO add-ons that will assist you in reaching that goal.

I Don't Have A Website Domain How Can I Get One?

We can offer you choices for the website domain 'www.yourbusinessname.com,' but there will be a small yearly charge based on the specific name. If you'd like to tackle this yourself we recommend namecheap.com - we will just need a few settings adjusted in your account at time of publishing the website to connect.

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out, and I can explain everything in detail. We want you to feel comfortable choosing us, and we understand that our low prices and quick turnaround may seem too good to be true.
